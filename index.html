Welcome!

In business since 1980, B.I.A. Landscaping is an industry leader in Montreal and its surrounding areas. We offer our expertise and ideas to satisfy all of your landscaping requirements to your entire satisfaction.

Our landscapers are experienced and trained in accordance to the highest standards in the industry and in compliance with workplace safety. Courteous and dependable, our professionals complete projects with passion and integrity.

Our company is a member of l’Association des Paysagistes Professionnels du Québec (APPQ) since 1993. We follow standards of execution established by the APPQ and meet its insurance and financial criteria. We also respect the warranties established by the APPQ (one year for plants and two years for construction).

We are recommended by Techo-Bloc, Permacon and Rinox, all manufacturers of excellent paver products. We are also highly recommended by Laniel Prodamex, the most complete supplier of landscaping products on the West Island of Montreal.
